About This Project

This is an open-source educational platform designed to help students learn organic chemistry through comprehensive guides, detailed explanations, and interactive learning materials.

The project covers all major organic chemistry topics including HaloAlkanes, Alcohols, Aldehydes, Ketones, Carboxylic Acids, Amines, and more. Each chapter provides in-depth information about compound properties, reactions, and practical applications.

This project is licensed under GPL v3. This means derivative works must remain open source, fostering collaboration and continuous improvement of educational resources.

We welcome contributions from educators, students, and developers. Whether you want to fix a typo, add new content, or improve the codebase, your contributions are highly appreciated!
